# Qafila: Silk & Sand - Project Manifest

## 1. Project Vision
Trade-management RPG where users manage a treasury, buy limited real estate, and run trade convoys. Historical Middle Eastern/Silk Road setting.

## 2. Core Economic Logic (The "Bazaar" Rules)
- **Currency:** Dinars (D).
- **Property Scarcity:** 10,000 total properties cap in the main city.
  - 8,000 Simple Houses: Low cost, entry-level.
  - 1,000 Merchant Lofts: Moderate cost, near Great Bazaar.
  - 500 Grand Villas: High cost, high prestige.
  - 500 Royal Castles: Ultra-rare, "Social Hub".
- **Secondary Market:** Once 10k units are sold, users trade with each other (Flipping).

## 3. Trade & Convoy Mechanics
- **Progression:** Unlock "Convoy Leader" at 250,000 D.
- **Management:** Balance Speed, Capacity, and Risk.
- **Routes:** Great Silk Route (Long), Amber Road (High Risk), Gulf Waters (Sea/Dhows), Uncharted Paths (Hidden).

## 4. Engagement & Interactivity
- **Random Events:** "Event Cards" (Sultan's wedding, Desert storms).
- **Assets/Inventory:** Guard Contracts, Speed Feed, Rare Artifacts.
- **Visuals:** Parchment, gold, sand tones. Juicy interaction animations.
